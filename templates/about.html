About Cruise Ship Crew Dictionary

It is widely known to anyone who has ever worked on a ship that ship crew have their own language. We come from all over the world and over time, all of our individual languages have helped shape our common "ship" language.

The objective of this dictionary is to finally centralize all of the idiosyncrasies of our shared language and give it legitimacy beyond the confines of the crew decks.
