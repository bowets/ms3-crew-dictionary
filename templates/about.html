About Cruise Ship Crew Dictionary

It is widely known to anyone who has ever worked on a ship, that ship crew have their own language. We come from all over the world, from different countries, cultures & languages. However, when we are onboard we are one ship, one culture, one crew & one language.

The objective of this dictionary is to finally centralize all of the great elements of our shared language and give it a voice beyond the confines of the crew decks.
